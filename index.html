Reclaiming our story: one student at a time.

Diverse stories. Told by us for us.

iFeminist empowers students by exposing them to unknown and underrepresented women in history as well as enabling them to conduct their own research. iFeminist encourages research on diverse women from around the world and throughout time to change the narrative regarding women's contributions to society. Each article contributes to a larger database of women in history. We are students aimed at changing the face of history by conducting our own research projects.
