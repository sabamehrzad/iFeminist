Our Team

Saba Mehrzad

Founder, Editor in Chief, and Head Software Developer

Saba is a junior at Syosset High School in Syosset, New York.

She first started studying women in history when she was accepted as an intern at the New Historia under Women's Studies Professor Dr. Walker. She is excited to learn more about the women that paved the way for her! Once she started studying Sedigheh Dolatabadi, she started to truly love, cherish, and appreciate her background because of Dolatabadi's amazing accomplishments. Inspired by The New Historia, Saba created iFeminist in order to allow her writers/readers to feel empowered by their identity and feels as though studying Iranian women in history did that for her.

Outside of iFeminist, Saba is the Editor in Chief of her school's Newspaper, the President of her school's Feminist Club, President of the Breaking Borders Club that aims to desegregate Long Island, NY, and the Founder and President of her school's Womxn in STEM Club. She is also the Founder and President of the Iranian American Society Teen Chapter to celebrate her culture and is a student teacher at her local Farsi school.

Saba's work under Dr. Walker focused on Iranian journalist and philanthropist, Sedigheh Dolatabadi. It is published here! Check it out on the Discoveries page.
