Open Positions



Research Team


Description:

Members of the Research Team are responsible for submitting articles regarding women in history every month.

Specific Responsibilities Include:

  • Being an active member of iFeminist (responding to editors, participating in Geneva home, etc.)
  • Writing and submitting high quality articles every month
  • Responding to edits on articles within 5 days of notification
  • Responding to and reaching out to editors frequently
  • Attending Community Events every 2 months, via Zoom
  • Active participant in the Geneva channel (emoji reactions, responding in threads, etc.)


    • Web Development Team


    Description:

    Members of the Development Team are responsible for coding pages for the iFeminist articles, creating new pages for the website (ex. Future blog, organizing research page), and uploading edits to Github.

    Specific Responsibilities Include:

  • Being an active member of iFeminist (responding to editors, participating in Geneva home, etc.)
  • Code articles three times a week onto website
  • Must be proficient in HTML, CSS, and Javascript
  • Must be able to push, pull, and navigate Github
  • Must be able to code collaboratively
  • Attending Community Events every 2 months, via Zoom
  • Active participant in the Geneva channel (emoji reactions, responding in threads, etc.)
